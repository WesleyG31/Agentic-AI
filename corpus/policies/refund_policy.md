# Refund and Returns Policy

*Effective: 2026-01-01 · Owner: Customer Support · ACME GmbH*

## Return Window

Customers may return items within a 30-day return window starting from the delivery date of the order. The delivery date recorded by the carrier in our order system is authoritative. Returns initiated after the 30-day window are not eligible for a refund under this policy; support agents may escalate borderline cases (for example, delivery-date disputes) to a supervisor, but must not promise a refund outside the window on their own authority. Statutory warranty rights for defective goods under German law remain unaffected.

## Damaged or Defective Items

If an item arrives damaged or is defective, the customer is entitled to a full refund including shipping costs. Support agents should ask the customer for photos of the damage and the packaging where practical, attach them to the ticket, and record the damage report against the order. The customer does not pay return shipping for damaged or defective items; a prepaid return label is issued from the order record. Where the customer prefers a replacement instead of a refund, a replacement may be offered at no additional cost, subject to stock availability.

## Approval Thresholds

Refunds over €500 require supervisor approval before they are executed. Agents must record the reason for the refund and the order reference in the approval request; supervisors respond within one business day. Refunds of €500 or less may be processed directly by the handling support agent, provided the case meets the conditions in this policy. Any deviation from policy (goodwill refunds, out-of-window exceptions) requires supervisor approval regardless of the amount.

## Refund Method and Timeline

All refunds go to the original payment method used for the order — refunds to a different account, card, or payout method are not permitted. Once a refund is executed, the amount is credited within 5-10 business days, depending on the customer's bank or payment provider. Agents should communicate this 5-10 business-day timeline explicitly to the customer when confirming a refund, and note that ACME cannot accelerate processing on the payment provider's side.

## Change-of-Mind Returns

Customers may also return items simply because they changed their mind, within the same 30-day window from delivery. For change-of-mind returns the following conditions apply: the item must be unused and in its original packaging with all accessories, and the customer pays return shipping. Once the returned item is received and inspected at the warehouse, the purchase price is refunded to the original payment method within 5-10 business days. Original outbound shipping costs are not refunded for change-of-mind returns. Items that show signs of use or arrive without original packaging may be refused or refunded at a reduced amount after supervisor review.

## Agent Checklist

Before processing any refund, agents should: verify the delivery date and confirm the request is within the 30-day window; classify the case (damaged/defective vs. change of mind); check whether the amount exceeds €500 and obtain supervisor approval if so; confirm the refund method is the original payment method; and inform the customer of the 5-10 business-day timeline. All steps must be documented in the linked support ticket.
