# Shipping Policy

*Effective: 2026-01-01 · Owner: Logistics · ACME GmbH*

## Shipping Options and Rates

ACME GmbH offers three shipping tiers. Standard shipping within Germany takes 3-5 business days and costs €4.95; it is free on orders over €50 (order value after discounts, before shipping). Express shipping within Germany takes 1-2 business days and costs €9.95. EU shipping to all other EU member states takes 5-8 business days and costs €12.95. Delivery times are counted in business days (Monday to Friday, excluding German public holidays) from the day the parcel is handed to the carrier, not from the order date. Orders placed before 14:00 CET on a business day are normally handed to the carrier the same day.

## Free-Shipping Threshold

The free-shipping threshold of €50 applies to Standard shipping within Germany only. Express and EU shipments are always charged at the listed rates regardless of order value. If a customer partially cancels an order and the remaining value falls below €50 before dispatch, the Standard shipping fee of €4.95 is applied to the revised order. Returns do not retroactively remove free shipping that was correctly granted at the time of purchase.

## Tracking and Delivery

Every shipment receives a tracking number, which is emailed to the customer when the parcel is handed to the carrier and is also visible on the order record. The delivery date confirmed by the carrier is stored on the order and serves as the start of the 30-day return window defined in the Refund and Returns Policy. If a customer reports a delivery problem, agents should first check the carrier tracking status linked from the order before taking further action.

## Lost-Parcel Procedure

A parcel is treated as potentially lost when tracking shows no movement for 5 business days, or when the maximum delivery time for the tier (5 business days Standard, 2 business days Express, 8 business days EU) has been exceeded by more than 3 business days without a delivery scan. In that case the support agent opens a carrier investigation from the order record; the carrier responds within 10 business days. While the investigation is open, the agent keeps the customer informed via the ticket. If the carrier confirms loss, or the investigation exceeds 10 business days without resolution, the customer chooses between a free replacement shipment or a full refund including shipping costs, processed under the Refund and Returns Policy (refunds over €500 require supervisor approval). Customers are never required to wait for the carrier's internal claim payout.

## Address Errors and Failed Delivery

If a parcel is returned to ACME because of an incorrect address supplied by the customer, the item price is refunded but the original shipping cost is not, and re-shipment is charged at the applicable tier rate. If the address error was caused by ACME or the carrier, re-shipment is free of charge. Customers can correct the delivery address free of charge any time before the parcel is handed to the carrier by contacting support.
