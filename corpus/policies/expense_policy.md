# Expense and Reimbursement Policy

*Effective: 2026-01-01 · Owner: Finance · ACME GmbH*

## Scope

This policy governs the reimbursement of business expenses incurred by ACME GmbH employees, including travel, meals, equipment, software, and training. Expenses must be necessary for business purposes, reasonable in amount, and compliant with this policy. Personal expenses are never reimbursable. All claims are submitted through the expense module in the HR portal and are paid out with the next regular payroll run after approval.

## Pre-Approval Threshold

Pre-approval is required for any single expense above €200. Employees must obtain written approval from their manager — via the pre-approval workflow in the expense module — before committing to the expense. Purchases above €200 made without pre-approval may be rejected or only partially reimbursed at Finance's discretion. Expenses of €200 or less do not require pre-approval but must still meet the reasonableness and business-purpose requirements of this policy. Splitting a purchase into several smaller transactions to avoid the €200 threshold is not permitted and is treated as a policy violation.

## Receipts

Receipts are mandatory for every expense claim, regardless of amount. A valid receipt shows the vendor, date, itemized amounts, and VAT where applicable; credit-card statements alone are not sufficient. Digital receipts (PDF or photo) uploaded to the expense module are accepted and originals do not need to be mailed in. If a receipt is lost, the employee must complete a missing-receipt declaration in the expense module; Finance accepts at most two such declarations per employee per year, and claims above €200 are not reimbursed without a receipt under any circumstances.

## Per-Diem for Business Travel

For domestic business travel within Germany, employees receive a domestic per-diem of €28/day for each full travel day (24 hours away from home and the regular workplace). Partial travel days — the day of departure and the day of return, or single-day trips of more than 8 hours — are compensated at the statutory reduced rate of €14. The per-diem covers meals and incidental costs; individual meal receipts are not reimbursed in addition to the per-diem. If meals are provided free of charge (for example, hotel breakfast included or a hosted lunch), the per-diem is reduced according to the statutory percentages. International per-diem rates follow the official rates published by the German Federal Ministry of Finance.

## Submission Deadline and Approval Flow

Expense claims must be submitted within 30 days of the expense date (for travel, within 30 days of the return date). Claims submitted later than 30 days may be rejected; exceptions require Finance approval and a written justification. Each submitted claim is reviewed first by the employee's manager and then by Finance. Incomplete claims are returned to the employee with a comment and must be corrected and resubmitted within 14 days. For questions about reimbursability, per-diem calculations, or the status of a claim, contact finance@acme.de before incurring the expense whenever possible.
