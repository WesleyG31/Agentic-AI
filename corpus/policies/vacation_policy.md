# Vacation and Annual Leave Policy

*Effective: 2026-01-01 · Owner: Human Resources · ACME GmbH*

## Annual Leave Entitlement

Every full-time employee of ACME GmbH is entitled to 28 vacation days per calendar year. The entitlement accrues from the first day of employment and is granted pro rata for employees who join or leave the company during the year, as well as for part-time staff based on their contracted weekly working days. Vacation days are working days (Monday to Friday); public holidays in Berlin do not count against the entitlement. Employees can view their current balance — total days, days used, and days remaining — at any time in the HR portal under "My Leave".

## Carry-Over Rule

Unused vacation days may be carried over into the following calendar year up to a maximum of 5 days. Carried-over days must be used by March 31 of the new year; any carry-over days not taken by that date expire without compensation. Days beyond the 5-day carry-over limit are forfeited at year end. Managers are expected to plan team schedules so that employees can realistically use their full entitlement within the year. Exceptions to the forfeiture rule are possible only in documented cases of long-term illness or parental leave, and require written approval from Human Resources.

## Requesting Vacation

All vacation requests must be submitted via the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance of the first day of leave. Each request requires manager approval before it is confirmed; an unapproved request does not entitle the employee to be absent. Managers should respond to requests within 5 working days. For absences of 10 or more consecutive working days, employees should coordinate with their manager before submitting the request to ensure adequate team coverage. Short-notice requests (less than 2 weeks) may be granted at the manager's discretion in exceptional circumstances, but employees should not book travel before approval is confirmed in the portal.

## Sick Leave

Sick leave is separate from vacation and does not reduce the 28-day annual leave entitlement. Employees who are unable to work due to illness must inform their manager before their normal start time on the first day of absence. A doctor's note (Arbeitsunfähigkeitsbescheinigung) is required from day 3 of any sick absence and must be uploaded to the HR portal or handed to HR without delay. If an employee falls ill during approved vacation and provides a doctor's note covering the affected days, those days are credited back to the vacation balance in accordance with German law.

## Questions and Contacts

For questions about leave balances, carry-over, or the request workflow, contact the HR team via the HR portal ticket function or email hr@acme.de. Managers with questions about approving or declining requests should consult the manager guide in the portal or contact their HR business partner. This policy applies to all ACME GmbH employees in Germany and is reviewed annually by Human Resources.
