# Employee FAQ — Vacation, Sick Leave, Expenses & IT

*Effective: 2026-01-01 · Owner: Human Resources · ACME GmbH*

## How many vacation days do I get per year?

Full-time employees receive 28 vacation days per year. Part-time entitlements are pro-rated based on contracted weekly hours. Your current balance is always visible in the HR portal under "My Leave".

## How do I book vacation?

Submit your request through the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance. Your manager must approve the request before the dates are confirmed. Requests submitted with less notice may be declined unless there are exceptional circumstances.

## Can I carry unused vacation days into the next year?

Yes, up to a maximum of 5 days may be carried over into the next calendar year. Carried-over days must be used by March 31, after which they expire without compensation.

## Does sick leave count against my vacation days?

No. Sick leave is tracked separately from vacation and does not reduce your 28-day entitlement. Notify your manager on the first day of absence and record the sick days in the HR portal.

## When do I need a doctor's note for sick leave?

A doctor's note is required from day 3 of any continuous sick-leave period. Upload the note to the HR portal or hand it to HR within the same week.

## Do I need approval before spending money on behalf of ACME?

Pre-approval is required for any expense above €200. Request approval from your manager in writing before committing to the spend. Expenses above the threshold without pre-approval may not be reimbursed.

## What are the rules for expense reimbursement?

Receipts are mandatory for every claim, regardless of amount. Submit your expense report within 30 days of the expense; late submissions may be rejected. The domestic per-diem for business travel within Germany is €28 per day, which covers meals and incidentals — no meal receipts are needed when claiming the per-diem.

## I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Password resets are handled by the IT helpdesk with identity verification — the helpdesk will confirm who you are before issuing a reset. Never share a password or a reset link with anyone, including colleagues.

## How do I request access to a system or application?

Access requests require approval from both your manager and the system owner. Submit the request via the IT helpdesk, naming the system and the access level you need. Access is granted on a least-privilege basis.

## Is multi-factor authentication required?

Yes. MFA is mandatory on all systems at ACME, with no exceptions. If MFA is not working on your device, contact the IT helpdesk immediately rather than working around it.

## I received a suspicious email. What should I do?

Do not click links or open attachments. Report the message to security@acme.de and delete it only after Security confirms receipt. If you already clicked a link or entered credentials, contact the IT helpdesk immediately so your password can be reset.

## Can I export customer data to answer a support case?

Support agents may not export customer PII without DPO approval. Work with customer data inside the approved support tools only, and route any GDPR deletion or subject-access request to the privacy team — deletion requests are honored within 30 days and subject-access requests are answered within one month.
