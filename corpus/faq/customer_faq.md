# Customer FAQ — Ordering, Shipping, Returns & Support

*Effective: 2026-01-01 · Owner: Customer Support · ACME GmbH*

## How long does standard shipping take within Germany, and what does it cost?

Standard shipping within Germany takes 3-5 business days and costs €4.95. Standard shipping is free on all orders over €50. Delivery times are counted from the day your order leaves our warehouse, which is normally the next business day after you place the order.

## Is there a faster shipping option?

Yes. Express shipping within Germany is delivered in 1-2 business days and costs €9.95. Express is available for all in-stock items ordered before 14:00 on a business day.

## Do you ship to other EU countries?

Yes. EU shipping takes 5-8 business days and costs €12.95 per order. At this time we ship only within Germany and the EU; we do not offer delivery outside the EU.

## How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you receive a tracking link by email. You can also log in to your ACME account, open "My Orders", and select the order number to see its current status (processing, shipped, or delivered) and the carrier tracking details.

## What is your return window?

You may return items within 30 days of the delivery date. The return window is based on the confirmed delivery date shown in your order tracking, not the order date.

## My item arrived damaged or defective. What happens now?

Contact support with your order number and, if possible, photos of the damage. Damaged or defective items qualify for a full refund including shipping costs. You do not pay return shipping for a damaged or defective item.

## I changed my mind. Can I still return the item?

Yes, within the 30-day return window, provided the item is unused and in its original packaging. For change-of-mind returns, the customer pays the return shipping cost. Once the item passes inspection at our warehouse, your refund is issued.

## How and when will I receive my refund?

Refunds always go to the original payment method and arrive within 5-10 business days after the refund is approved. We cannot redirect refunds to a different card or account.

## Why is my larger refund taking a little longer to approve?

Refunds over €500 require supervisor approval before they can be processed. This internal check usually adds no more than one business day; the 5-10 business day payout window then applies as normal.

## Can I cancel or change an order after placing it?

If the order has not yet shipped, contact support as soon as possible and we will try to cancel or amend it. Once an order has shipped, please use the standard 30-day return process instead.

## How do I contact customer support?

Email support@acme.de or use the contact form in your account. Please include your order number (for example, "Order 4471") in the subject line so we can route your request quickly. Support responds on business days, and damaged-item reports are treated as high priority.

## How do you handle my personal data if I ask for it to be deleted?

Under GDPR, deletion requests are honored within 30 days, and subject-access requests are answered within one month. Send either request to support and it will be handled through our data-protection process.
